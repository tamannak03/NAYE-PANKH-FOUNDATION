About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a student-led non-profit organization registered under the Government of Uttar Pradesh with 80G and 12A certifications. We are committed to uplifting underprivileged communities through education support, menstrual hygiene awareness, food distribution, animal welfare, youth engagement, and volunteer-driven social impact initiatives across India. With a network of thousands of changemakers, we strive to build a society where every individual has equal opportunities to grow and thrive.
